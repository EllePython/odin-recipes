Lasagna Recipe

Description

Delicious Lasgna recipe passed down from generation to generation. boasting layers of rich flavors and textures that come together in a harmonious blend. Picture this mouthwatering dish:

Each slice is a symphony of flavors, with the layers holding together beautifully. Served piping hot, the lasagna is plated with a dollop of ricotta on top, a drizzle of marinara sauce, and a sprinkle of fresh herbs for a final touch of culinary finesse. In essence, a delicious lasagna is a comforting, flavor-packed experience that brings together the best elements of Italian cuisine in each glorious bite.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmessan Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Peppers (Any Color)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Paprika
  • Chicken Strock

Steps

  1. Dice onions and peppers
  2. Saute onions and peppers
  3. Add ground beef to cook with saute veggies
  4. Add seasoning 1tbps of each onto beef while cooking
  5. Once the ground beef is cooked add chicken broth and tomato sauce
  6. Bring saltwater to a boil and then add lasagna noodles (al-dente)
  7. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  8. Mix in a bowl all 3 cheeses
  9. In a baking pan layer noodles and beef while adding cheese mix on top
  10. Final/Top layer add only cheese mix
  11. Bake for 45 mins
