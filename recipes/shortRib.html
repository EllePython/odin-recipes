Short Rib

Description

Succulent short ribs, seasoned and seared to perfection, then slow-cooked until tender in a savory blend of broth, red wine, and aromatic herbs. Served over creamy mashed potatoes, the dish is a hearty symphony of rich flavors and fall-off-the-bone goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 5 pounds bone-in short ribs
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 large garlic heads
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 celery
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 tbs tomato paste
  • 2 cups dry red wine
  • 2 cups beef stock or bone broth
  • 4 sprigs thyme
  • 1 cup parsley
  • 1/2 finely chopped chives
  • 1 tbs finely grated lemon zest

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 275 degrees. Heat oil in large pan over medium-high heat
  2. Season short ribs on all side with salt and pepper
  3. Sear the short ribs until all sides are evenly browned
  4. Reduce heat to medium add garlic, cut side down and cook until golden brown
  5. Add onion, celery, carrots and season with salt and pepper. Continue to cook until vegestable are softened but not yet browned
  6. Add tomato paste and stor to coat until tomato paste has caramelize a bit o the bottom and edges of the pot
  7. Add red wine and scrape up any browned/caramelized bits
  8. Let simmer for 2/3 minutes with beef stock and thyme. Then return short ribs to pan, ensure they are submerged
  9. Bring to a simmer, then cover, and transfer to oven
  10. Cook, undisturbed until short ribs are tender and fall off bone
  11. Pair with veggies or mashed potatoes, Enjoy!!
